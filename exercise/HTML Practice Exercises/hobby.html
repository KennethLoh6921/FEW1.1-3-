Badminton is a racquet sport played with a shuttlecock and rackets. It can be played by two (singles) or four (doubles) players on a rectangular court divided by a net. The objective is to score points by hitting the shuttlecock over the net and landing it within the opponent's half of the court, while preventing them from doing the same.
